# Small Business AI Starter Packet v1

**Product:** AIRegReady Small Business AI Starter Packet  
**Version:** 1.0 draft  
**Intended price:** cheap first offer — **$19 for the packet**, or **$7 per document** separately  
**Audience:** small businesses, solo founders, nonprofits, and small teams that use ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Gemini, or similar AI tools at work.

## What this packet is

A small, practical starter packet for teams that need basic AI guardrails without buying a heavy compliance program.

It gives a small team three useful documents:

1. **One-Page AI Use Rules** — plain-English staff rules for safe AI use.
2. **AI Tool Inventory** — a simple record of which AI tools the team uses, who owns them, and what data goes in.
3. **New AI Tool Approval Checklist** — a quick triage form before approving a new AI tool.

## What this packet is not

This is not legal advice, not a compliance guarantee, and not a substitute for qualified counsel. It is a practical educational template pack.

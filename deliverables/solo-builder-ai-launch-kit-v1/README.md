# Solo Builder AI Launch Kit v1

**Product:** Solo Builder AI Launch Kit
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Prepared:** 2026-06-08

This packet contains editable templates and reference PDFs for solo builders documenting AI-enabled apps, websites, automations, consulting workflows, client deliverables, digital products, productized services, and micro-SaaS tools before launch.

Start with `START-HERE.md`, then adapt the DOCX files for your project.

## Included documents

1. **AI Project Snapshot** - captures the basic facts about the AI project, AI role, data inputs, claims, and review date.
2. **AI Claims Checklist** - helps review public claims, evidence, safer rewrites, and publish decisions.
3. **User Data and Privacy Intake** - maps data collected, data sent to AI, vendor exposure, logs, and do-not-submit rules.
4. **AI Disclosure and Disclaimer Builder** - provides starter disclosure, limitation, review, data-use, and problem-reporting language.
5. **Red Flag Use Case Triage** - helps sort lower-risk, needs-review, and pause/escalate AI uses.
6. **Vendor / Model Dependency Note** - records vendors, models, prompts, settings, limitations, and fallback plans.
7. **Pre-Launch AI Risk Checklist** - supports a practical ship/no-ship decision before launch.
8. **Customer FAQ Template** - prepares plain answers about AI use, data, review, limitations, and reporting problems.
9. **Incident / Complaint Mini-Log** - tracks bad outputs, user confusion, data concerns, and follow-up actions.
10. **AI Change Log** - tracks model, prompt, vendor, data, disclosure, claim, and behavior changes.
11. **30-Minute Setup Guide** - gives the fastest useful path through the packet.
12. **Filled Example: AI Website or App** - shows a completed record for a user-facing AI app.
13. **Filled Example: Consultant or Freelancer Using AI** - shows a completed record for AI-assisted client work.
14. **Filled Example: Digital Product, Automation, or Micro-SaaS** - shows a completed record for a small AI product or workflow.

## Folder structure

- `markdown/` - plain-text source/reference files
- `docx/` - editable Word-compatible documents
- `pdf/` - PDF exports
- `START-HERE.md` - buyer-facing setup guide
- `LEGAL-NOTICE.md` - educational-use and legal-boundary notice
- `source-notes/` - notes identifying source drafts used to assemble the packet

## Important boundary

These materials are educational templates and practical documentation starting points only. They are not legal advice, do not create an attorney-client relationship, do not make an AI project compliant, do not approve a use case, do not guarantee safety, and do not replace qualified legal, privacy, security, HR, procurement, platform, or sector-specific review.

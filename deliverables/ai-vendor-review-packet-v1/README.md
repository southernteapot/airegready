# AI Vendor Review Packet v1

**Product:** AI Vendor Review Packet
**Version:** 1.1.0
**Prepared:** 2026-06-05
**Updated:** 2026-06-09

This packet contains editable templates and reference PDFs for reviewing AI vendors, SaaS AI features, embedded AI tools, and new AI-enabled workflows before approval. Start with `START-HERE.md`, then adapt the DOCX files for your organization.

## Included documents

1. **AI Vendor Review Request Email Template**
   Email and message templates for requesting AI, privacy, security, data-use, and contract information from a vendor.

2. **AI Procurement Checklist Lite**
   A 15-30 minute triage checklist for low-stakes AI tools and early vendor review.

3. **AI Vendor Risk Assessment Questionnaire**
   A full questionnaire covering purpose, data, use case, vendor basics, model training, data handling, security, AI-specific controls, legal/contractual terms, and exit planning.

4. **AI Vendor Scoring Guide**
   A scoring and tiering guide for classifying a vendor/use case as Low, Medium, High, or Unacceptable, with approval conditions.

5. **AI Vendor Red Flag List**
   A practical checklist of issues that should pause, reject, or escalate a vendor review.

6. **AI Vendor Decision Memo and Approval Record**
   A decision record template for documenting approval, approval conditions, rejection rationale, owner, scope, and next review date.

### Bonus materials

- **Bonus Filled Example: Completed Vendor Review**
  A fictional end-to-end example showing questionnaire highlights, red-flag handling, and a filled decision memo with conditions.

## Folder structure

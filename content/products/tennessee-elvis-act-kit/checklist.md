# Tennessee ELVIS Act — Readiness Checklist

**Organization:** ______________________________
**Completed by:** ______________________________

## Phase 1 — Scope

- [ ] **1.1** Identified whether your products or services can produce voice / likeness content of real persons
- [ ] **1.2** Identified whether your products or services host user-generated voice / likeness content
- [ ] **1.3** Assessed reach to Tennessee consumers / jurisdictions

## Phase 2 — Consent

- [ ] **2.1** Consent workflow for using named individuals' voice / likeness
- [ ] **2.2** Specific-use consent (not a broad waiver)
- [ ] **2.3** Posthumous use consent from estate / heirs
- [ ] **2.4** Records of consent retained

## Phase 3 — Product controls

- [ ] **3.1** Terms of Service prohibit unauthorized use
- [ ] **3.2** Training data review: no unauthorized celebrity voices
- [ ] **3.3** Generation guardrails: refuse to clone identified real persons absent verified consent
- [ ] **3.4** Celebrity / public-figure detection
- [ ] **3.5** User-uploaded-voice verification workflow

## Phase 4 — Platform / UGC

- [ ] **4.1** Takedown process for reported unauthorized content
- [ ] **4.2** Repeat-infringer policy
- [ ] **4.3** Response SLA
- [ ] **4.4** Transparency reporting (optional but useful)

## Phase 5 — Marketing

- [ ] **5.1** No celebrity / real-person voice in ads without consent
- [ ] **5.2** AI-generated spokespersons reviewed for real-person similarity
- [ ] **5.3** Influencer AI content reviewed for real-person use

## Phase 6 — Vendor / model relationships

- [ ] **6.1** Vendor contracts prohibit unauthorized use
- [ ] **6.2** Vendor controls documented
- [ ] **6.3** Vendor disclosure of training data where feasible

## Phase 7 — Incident response

- [ ] **7.1** Takedown received — process within 24 hours
- [ ] **7.2** Legal escalation for claims
- [ ] **7.3** Customer / user notification as appropriate

## Phase 8 — Records

- [ ] **8.1** Consent forms retained
- [ ] **8.2** Takedown history
- [ ] **8.3** Incident records

## Phase 9 — Interaction with other laws

- [ ] **9.1** California digital-replica rules
- [ ] **9.2** Other state right-of-publicity laws
- [ ] **9.3** EU AI Act Art 50 disclosure for deepfakes
- [ ] **9.4** Federal proposals monitored

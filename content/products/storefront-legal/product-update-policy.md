# AIRegReady — Product Update Policy

**Effective date:** [DATE]

This policy explains what "12 months of free updates" means, how updates are delivered, and what happens after the free period ends.

---

## What's included

Your Product purchase includes **twelve (12) months of free updates** beginning on the date of purchase. During that period, you get every update we publish for the purchased Product.

Updates can include:

- **Regulatory updates** — when a law or rule that the Product references changes
- **Scope expansions** — new sections, templates, or appendices added to the Product
- **Quality improvements** — clarifications, typo fixes, better examples
- **Format additions** — if we release new formats (e.g., a Google Doc version), you get access to them
- **Bundled additions** — if we add a related template to a kit you already own, you get it

## How updates are delivered

- **By email**. The address on your order receives an update notice with a download link.
- **On your customer portal** (if used). Your purchase history shows available updates.
- **Version numbers**. Each Product has a version number (e.g., v1.3). Updates bump the version.

If the email on your order is stale, email [SUPPORT EMAIL] with your order number and new email address; we'll update our records.

## What's not an "update"

- A **new Product** (with a new name and scope) is not an update. Owners of related Products may get a discount, but they don't get the new Product free.
- A **major v2** release is considered a new Product. You retain access to your version (e.g., v1.7) indefinitely. We typically offer a v1 → v2 upgrade at a discount.

## Notification cadence

We don't spam. You'll hear from us when:

1. A Product you own is updated
2. A law the Product references changes materially
3. A related Product or bundle is released that might interest you
4. There's a security or safety issue with the Product we shipped

Expect a few emails per year per Product — not weekly.

## After the 12-month free period

Your original Product remains usable indefinitely. To continue receiving updates, you can:

- **Renew updates for the Product** for [$X / year per product]
- **Subscribe to the Regulatory Update Service** for [$Y / month], which covers all Products you own (and any new Products you buy while subscribed)
- **Do nothing**. You keep the Product version you have.

## Regulatory Update Service

Our Regulatory Update Service (optional, separately priced):

- Extends free updates beyond the initial 12 months
- Sends a monthly briefing on regulatory changes across the AI landscape (EU, U.S. federal, state-by-state, sector)
- Includes access to a private change-log for Products, showing what changed and why
- Gives early access to new Products

Cancel any time; access continues through the end of the paid period.

## Update scope for kits

Kits that include multiple Products (e.g., Colorado AI Act Readiness Kit) are updated as a unit. Any component update triggers an update notification.

## Update scope for bundles

If you bought a bundle, updates for each Product in the bundle are included until the bundle's 12-month update window expires.

## Version archive

We retain the last [3] prior versions of each Product and make them available on request. If you built organizational policies against a specific version, let us know and we'll preserve access.

## Reseller updates

Reseller License holders receive updates continuously during the License term, per the Reseller License. End-client engagements already delivered are the Reseller's responsibility to update.

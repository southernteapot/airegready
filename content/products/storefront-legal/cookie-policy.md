## How to control cookies

- **Browser controls**: most browsers let you block or delete cookies from the browser settings. Blocking strictly necessary cookies may break parts of the Site.
- **Our cookie banner**: where required (e.g., for EU/UK visitors), we present a banner to accept or decline optional cookies.
- **Do Not Track**: we honor the Global Privacy Control signal where applicable law requires.

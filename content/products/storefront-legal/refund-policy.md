## The short version

Digital products are delivered instantly and can't be "returned" in the usual sense. We still want you to be happy with what you bought. If a Product doesn't meet the description on the sales page, **email us at [SUPPORT EMAIL] within 14 days of purchase** and we'll work it out — usually a refund, sometimes a replacement or a credit.

## When refunds are available

We will refund your purchase if:

- The Product described on the sales page is materially different from what you received
- A download link failed and we couldn't resolve it within a reasonable time
- You were charged in error (duplicate charge, wrong product, technical glitch)
- You purchased a Product that is covered by an applicable consumer-protection withdrawal right (e.g., certain EU / UK consumer purchases) and you exercise that right within the statutory window before you download the Product

## When refunds are not available

Because digital products can't be returned:

- We generally do not offer refunds after you have downloaded the Product
- We do not offer refunds for change of mind, misreading the sales page, or deciding the Product isn't needed
- We do not offer partial refunds for individual items in a bundle
- Subscription products (e.g., the Regulatory Update Subscription) follow a separate refund rule — see Section 5 below

## How to request a refund

- Email [SUPPORT EMAIL] within **14 days** of purchase
- Include your order number, the Product name, and a short description of what's wrong
- We respond within [3 business days]

If a refund is approved, we'll process it to the original payment method. Refunds typically appear in 5–10 business days, depending on your card issuer or bank.

## EU / UK / California statutory rights

Consumers in some jurisdictions have additional statutory rights. Nothing in this policy waives those rights.

For **EU / UK consumers**: the Consumer Rights Directive generally gives consumers a 14-day withdrawal right for online purchases. Because our Products are digital content delivered immediately upon download, we ask you to acknowledge at checkout that you understand you are waiving the 14-day withdrawal right once you begin the download. If you have not yet downloaded and you are within 14 days of purchase, we will refund.

For **California consumers**: you have rights under the California Consumer Protection Act (CCPA/CPRA). This refund policy does not affect those rights.

## Bundles and upgrades

- **Bundles** are priced as a single Product. Partial refunds for individual items inside a bundle are not available.
- **Upgrade purchases** (e.g., upgrading from a single product to a bundle): the upgrade fee is refundable under the same rules as a new purchase.

## Subscription products

Our subscription products (e.g., Regulatory Update Subscription, Enterprise Updates):

- You may cancel at any time
- On cancellation, you keep access for the remainder of the billing period you paid for
- We do not pro-rate refunds for unused portions of the current period
- If the subscription was charged in error or duplicated, email us and we'll make it right

## Reseller licenses

Reseller licenses are custom commercial agreements. Refund rules for Reseller Licenses are described in the Reseller License document and may differ from this policy.

## Chargebacks

If you dispute a charge with your bank or credit card issuer without first contacting us, we may be required to spend time responding to the dispute process. We would much rather just solve the problem. Please email [SUPPORT EMAIL] first.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The version in effect at the time of your purchase governs your order.

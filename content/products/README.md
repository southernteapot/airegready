# AIRegReady Products — Working Drafts

Internal working drafts of paid products. Not published to the public site.

Each product lives in its own folder. Drafts are written in plain-English markdown with `[BRACKETED PLACEHOLDERS]` for the buyer to fill in. At delivery time we convert to Word + PDF (and optionally a Google Doc copy).

## Product catalog

### Core policies
- **ai-acceptable-use-policy** — Rules for how employees may use AI tools at work. Broadest audience; likely the first paid product.
- **ai-governance-framework** — Umbrella document establishing roles, committees, and review processes for AI use across a company.
- **ai-incident-response-plan** — Playbook for responding when an AI system misbehaves, leaks data, or causes harm.
- **ai-data-governance-policy** — Policy for data used in AI training, fine-tuning, prompts, and outputs.

### Assessments & inventories
- **ai-vendor-risk-assessment** — Questionnaire and scoring guide for evaluating AI vendors before purchase or renewal.
- **ai-procurement-checklist-lite** — Fast-triage version of the vendor assessment for low-stakes tools.
- **ai-system-inventory** — Template for tracking every AI system in use at a company.
- **ai-impact-assessment** — Fill-in impact assessment for Tier 2 / Tier 3 deployments.
- **shadow-ai-discovery-playbook** — Humane process for finding unapproved AI tools already in use.

### Jurisdictional readiness kits
- **colorado-ai-act-readiness-kit** — Overview + checklist + impact assessment template for Colorado AI Act.
- **eu-ai-act-high-risk-kit** — Overview + checklist for high-risk providers and deployers under the EU AI Act.
- **texas-traiga-readiness-kit** — Overview + checklist for the Texas Responsible AI Governance Act.
- **california-ai-readiness-kit** — Consolidated overview + checklist covering TFAIA, SB 942, AB 2013, CCPA ADMT, SB 1001, AB 2655, and sector rules.
- **nyc-local-law-144-pack** — Overview + bias audit brief + candidate notice for LL 144 AEDT compliance.
- **nist-ai-rmf-implementation-pack** — Overview + checklist + control mapping for NIST AI RMF.

### Sector kits
- **ai-in-healthcare-kit** — HIPAA + FDA SaMD + Section 1557 + state rules.
- **ai-in-hr-hiring-pack** — Consolidated HR pack: overview + policy addendum + hiring workflow.

### Customer-facing templates
- **customer-ai-disclosure-templates** — Website, chatbot, email, terms addendum, hiring notice, adverse action.

### Training & enablement
- **ai-employee-training-program** — Outline + 28-slide deck + quiz + attestation + facilitator guide.
- **ai-quick-reference-cards** — One-pagers by role: engineer, marketer, HR, sales, support, executive.

### Leadership
- **ai-board-report-template** — Quarterly AI posture report template + instructions.

## Licensing assumption (first pass)

- Single-company, perpetual license
- Customers may modify for internal use
- Customers may **not** redistribute, resell, or use for consulting clients without a separate reseller license
- 12 months of free updates included
- Upgrade path to enterprise and reseller licenses

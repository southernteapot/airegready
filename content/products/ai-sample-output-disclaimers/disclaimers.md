# AI Sample Output Disclaimer Templates

Short, ready-to-paste disclaimers to show alongside AI-generated content in your product or publications. Choose based on context; adapt tone.

---

## General-purpose in-product disclaimers

### 1 — Minimal (under an AI-generated response)

> *AI can make mistakes — verify important information.*

### 2 — Slightly more detailed

> *This response was generated by an AI assistant. It may contain errors. Review before relying on it for decisions.*

### 3 — With a feedback loop

> *AI-generated. If this looks wrong, let us know — [feedback button] — and we'll improve.*

### 4 — Contextual (search / research)

> *Summarized by AI from [N] sources. [See sources] · [Verify key details]*

### 5 — Code assistant

> *AI-generated code. Review, test, and apply security best practices before using.*

### 6 — Coding autocomplete

> *AI suggestion — review before accepting.*

### 7 — Image

> *Illustration generated with AI.*

### 8 — Long-form content (blog / article)

> *Note: This article was drafted with the assistance of AI tools and reviewed by a human editor before publication.*

### 9 — For any output shown to a child user

> *You are chatting with an AI assistant. Ask a grown-up for help if you're not sure about something.*

## Sector-specific disclaimers

### Health / medical information

> *This information is generated by an AI assistant for educational purposes only. It is not medical advice and does not replace consultation with a qualified healthcare professional. If you have a medical concern, please contact a doctor.*

### Legal information

> *AI-generated content for general information only. Not legal advice. Consult a licensed attorney in your jurisdiction for advice about your specific situation.*

### Financial information

> *AI-generated content for educational purposes. Not financial, investment, or tax advice. Consult a qualified financial professional before making decisions.*

### Safety-critical instructions

> *Do not rely solely on this AI-generated information for safety-critical decisions. Follow manufacturer instructions and consult a qualified professional.*

## Chatbot / voice agent disclosures

### Short chat opener

> *Hi, I'm [BOT NAME], an AI assistant from [COMPANY]. How can I help?*

### Longer chat opener

> *👋 I'm [BOT NAME], an AI assistant from [COMPANY]. I can help with [SCOPE]. I'm not a human and I sometimes get things wrong — if you'd like a person, type "agent".*

### Voice assistant opener

> *Hi, this is [NAME], an AI assistant from [COMPANY]. This call may be recorded. Press or say "agent" to reach a person.*

### Handoff to human

> *Connecting you to a team member now.*

### Handoff from human to AI

> *I'll take it from here — I'm [BOT NAME], an AI assistant.*

## Adverse-action-adjacent

### Preliminary

> *This is a preliminary result generated by an AI. A human reviewer will review and finalize.*

### Final with appeal

> *This decision was made with the help of an AI tool. You have the right to request review by a human. [Request review]*

## Public-facing AI-created content (articles, videos)

### Editorial

> *This article includes content generated with AI tools. A human editor reviewed and edited before publication.*

### News (non-editorial)

> *Portions of this article were assisted by AI summarization tools. Facts and quotes were verified by [REPORTER / DESK].*

### Video / audio with AI generation

> *This [video / audio] contains AI-generated [voice / image / animation].*

### Fully synthetic

> *This is fully AI-generated media. Nothing shown depicts real events or people unless otherwise noted.*

## Internal / employee-facing disclaimers

### On internal AI-drafted docs

> *This draft was produced with AI assistance. Review before circulating externally.*

### On AI-generated meeting summaries

> *Summary generated by AI. May contain errors or omissions. Refer to the recording / source material for anything important.*

## Compliance / regulatory (specific purposes)

### Adverse action (AI contributed)

> *This decision was made with the assistance of an automated system. You have the right to an explanation of the principal factors, to correct information used, and to request human review. Contact: [PHONE / EMAIL].*

### High-risk AI deployment notice (EU AI Act / CAIA)

> *You are about to use a system that uses artificial intelligence to [PURPOSE]. [COMPANY] has evaluated this system for accuracy and fairness. You may request human review of any decision that materially affects you. Details: [URL].*

### Synthetic content provenance

> *This content includes synthetic elements. Provenance: [C2PA / method] signed by [COMPANY].*

## Design considerations

- **Prominence**: disclaimers should be visible without hunting
- **Tone**: match the product; don't scare users away
- **Consistency**: use the same disclosure language across surfaces
- **Localization**: translate accurately; legal meaning should survive
- **Accessibility**: screen-reader-accessible text, not embedded in images alone

## Governance

- Maintain this list in a shared brand / legal library
- Legal reviews changes
- Annual review
- Track which disclaimer appears where (for change management and audit)

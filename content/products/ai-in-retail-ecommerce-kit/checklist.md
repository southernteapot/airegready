# AI in Retail & E-commerce — Readiness Checklist

**Company:** ______________________________
**Completed by:** ______________________________
**Date:** ______________________________

---

## Phase 1 — Scope

- [ ] **1.1** AI System Inventory covers search, recommendations, pricing, chatbots, fraud, inventory, marketing, in-store analytics, customer service
- [ ] **1.2** Each system tagged for applicable rules (FTC fake review, dark patterns, PCI, COPPA, CA AADC, BIPA, state biometric, CA SB 942, bot disclosure)

## Phase 2 — Reviews and testimonials

- [ ] **2.1** No AI-generated reviews posted as organic
- [ ] **2.2** Review incentive disclosures comply with FTC Endorsement Guides
- [ ] **2.3** Review suppression policies reviewed (no suppressing negative reviews without cause)
- [ ] **2.4** Employee / insider review disclosures enforced
- [ ] **2.5** AI detection of fake reviews in place

## Phase 3 — Dynamic pricing

- [ ] **3.1** Personalization inputs reviewed for protected-class proxies
- [ ] **3.2** Transparent pricing policy
- [ ] **3.3** State surge-pricing / price-gouging rules monitored
- [ ] **3.4** Antitrust review for shared pricing AI or consortium participation

## Phase 4 — Dark patterns

- [ ] **4.1** UX / UI audit for manipulative patterns (hidden fees, confirmshaming, nagging, forced continuity)
- [ ] **4.2** Subscription signup / cancellation flows comply with ROSCA + state rules
- [ ] **4.3** AI-driven retention tactics reviewed against FTC guidance

## Phase 5 — Chatbots and customer service

- [ ] **5.1** AI disclosure in chat / voice
- [ ] **5.2** "Are you human?" handled honestly
- [ ] **5.3** Price, availability, and policy statements verified before bots make them
- [ ] **5.4** Accessibility (screen readers, captions, alt channels)
- [ ] **5.5** Escalation to humans available

## Phase 6 — Fraud AI

- [ ] **6.1** Threshold and escalation rules documented
- [ ] **6.2** False-positive remediation path (customer recovery, recovery time SLA)
- [ ] **6.3** Logs and audit trails preserved
- [ ] **6.4** Appeal process for locked accounts
- [ ] **6.5** Biometric inputs comply with state law

## Phase 7 — In-store analytics

- [ ] **7.1** Consent and signage for biometric identifiers where required (BIPA, others)
- [ ] **7.2** Data retention and deletion schedules
- [ ] **7.3** Vendor DPAs for analytics providers
- [ ] **7.4** No inference of protected classes
- [ ] **7.5** Employee notice for workplace analytics

## Phase 8 — Personalization and profiling

- [ ] **8.1** CCPA/CPRA ADMT notices, opt-outs, and access rights
- [ ] **8.2** GDPR profiling rules where applicable
- [ ] **8.3** Sensitive inferences avoided or handled with consent
- [ ] **8.4** Children's data protections applied

## Phase 9 — AI-generated content on site

- [ ] **9.1** Product descriptions reviewed for accuracy (spec hallucinations are common)
- [ ] **9.2** AI imagery disclosure where authorship matters (editorial)
- [ ] **9.3** CA SB 942 provenance applied where threshold met

## Phase 10 — Recommendations

- [ ] **10.1** Accessibility tested
- [ ] **10.2** Bias / fairness spot-checks
- [ ] **10.3** Age-appropriate recommendations for child users
- [ ] **10.4** Algorithmic transparency disclosed where required (DSA for large platforms)

## Phase 11 — Payments / PCI

- [ ] **11.1** AI systems handling payment card data comply with PCI DSS
- [ ] **11.2** Tokenization and segmentation applied
- [ ] **11.3** Vendor PCI attestations on file

## Phase 12 — Training and governance

- [ ] **12.1** Marketing, customer service, and operations trained
- [ ] **12.2** AI Governance Framework applied
- [ ] **12.3** Complaint channel includes AI-related concerns

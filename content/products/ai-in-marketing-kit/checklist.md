# AI in Marketing — Readiness Checklist

**Company / Brand:** [NAME]
**Completed by:** ______________________________
**Date:** ______________________________

---

## Phase 1 — Scope

- [ ] **1.1** AI System Inventory covers every marketing AI tool (content generation, ad ops, targeting, personalization, SEO, social, email, influencer platforms, analytics)
- [ ] **1.2** Agencies and influencer partners inventoried for AI use
- [ ] **1.3** Risk tiers assigned

## Phase 2 — Claims review

- [ ] **2.1** Every AI-related marketing claim substantiated with evidence
- [ ] **2.2** "AI-powered," "patented AI," "proprietary AI" terminology cleared by legal and engineering
- [ ] **2.3** Benchmarks and performance claims documented
- [ ] **2.4** Claims-review workflow required before publishing AI-mentioning content
- [ ] **2.5** Corrections procedure for claims found wanting

## Phase 3 — Endorsements and testimonials

- [ ] **3.1** Endorsement policy aligned with FTC Endorsement Guides
- [ ] **3.2** No fabricated or AI-generated testimonials
- [ ] **3.3** Material connections disclosed (paid, free product, employee)
- [ ] **3.4** Influencer contracts require disclosure of AI-assisted content
- [ ] **3.5** Case studies use verified customer quotes and data

## Phase 4 — Synthetic media

- [ ] **4.1** Written Synthetic Media Policy
- [ ] **4.2** Disclosure applied per CA SB 942, EU AI Act Art 50, TX TRAIGA
- [ ] **4.3** Machine-readable provenance (C2PA / watermark) applied where feasible
- [ ] **4.4** Consent obtained for any identifiable person depicted
- [ ] **4.5** Restricted categories (public figures, minors, sensitive contexts) escalated to legal
- [ ] **4.6** Creative tools restricted to approved vendors

## Phase 5 — Right of publicity

- [ ] **5.1** Pre-publication screen for content featuring real people
- [ ] **5.2** Voice cloning only with consent; Tennessee ELVIS Act checked
- [ ] **5.3** Employee and customer likenesses used only with written release
- [ ] **5.4** Deceased persons: estate rights checked in relevant states

## Phase 6 — Targeting and personalization

- [ ] **6.1** Protected-class segmentation audit (housing, credit, employment, healthcare ads)
- [ ] **6.2** Lookalike model inputs reviewed for bias proxies
- [ ] **6.3** Dynamic pricing reviewed for disparate impact
- [ ] **6.4** Sensitive inferences (health, political, religious) have legal basis; consent where required
- [ ] **6.5** CCPA/CPRA ADMT + GDPR profiling requirements met

## Phase 7 — Bot disclosure

- [ ] **7.1** All chatbots display AI identity before or at the start of interaction
- [ ] **7.2** "Are you human?" responses configured honestly
- [ ] **7.3** Voice agents include AI-voice disclosure per CA AB 2905 and similar
- [ ] **7.4** Political bots comply with SB 1001 and state rules

## Phase 8 — TCPA / CAN-SPAM

- [ ] **8.1** AI-generated emails comply with CAN-SPAM (accurate headers, clear unsubscribe)
- [ ] **8.2** AI voice calls have prior express written consent for marketing
- [ ] **8.3** SMS outreach complies with TCPA and state rules
- [ ] **8.4** Opt-out honored across all channels

## Phase 9 — IP and copyright

- [ ] **9.1** AI content pipeline documented for provenance
- [ ] **9.2** Training data licenses for custom models documented
- [ ] **9.3** Trademark check applied to AI-generated identifiers / logos / names
- [ ] **9.4** Fair use / license defensibility assessed for significant AI outputs
- [ ] **9.5** Vendor indemnification reviewed

## Phase 10 — Governance

- [ ] **10.1** Marketing AI aligned to AI Governance Framework
- [ ] **10.2** Training for in-house marketing team
- [ ] **10.3** Agency contracts require AI policy alignment
- [ ] **10.4** Complaints / feedback channel for AI-related concerns

## Phase 11 — Incident handling

- [ ] **11.1** Content retraction workflow for incorrect AI claims
- [ ] **11.2** Consumer complaint handling
- [ ] **11.3** Regulator inquiry response process
- [ ] **11.4** Post-incident review and policy updates

## Phase 12 — Records

- [ ] **12.1** Content production records (who, what tool, when, what inputs, what approvals)
- [ ] **12.2** Endorsement and testimonial verification records
- [ ] **12.3** Targeting audit records
- [ ] **12.4** Consent and release records for likeness/voice

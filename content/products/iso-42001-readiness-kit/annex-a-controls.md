# ISO/IEC 42001 — Annex A Controls Reference

A summary of Annex A controls with implementation prompts. The standard itself contains the authoritative text; this document helps you turn the standard into practice.

For each control:

- **Objective** — what the control aims to achieve
- **Implementation prompt** — what "good" looks like
- **Evidence examples** — what auditors typically want to see

---

## A.2 — Policies related to AI

### A.2.2 AI policy

- **Objective**: Establish organizational policy for developing and/or using AI systems
- **Implementation**: Written AI policy, approved by top management, covering scope, principles, responsibilities, and management commitments
- **Evidence**: Signed policy document; evidence of communication to staff

### A.2.3 Alignment with other organizational policies

- **Objective**: AI policy is consistent with other policies (security, privacy, ethics)
- **Implementation**: Review AI policy against existing policy suite; reconcile conflicts
- **Evidence**: Policy reconciliation memo; cross-references

### A.2.4 Review of AI policy

- **Objective**: AI policy reviewed at planned intervals
- **Implementation**: Schedule annual review; ad hoc review on material change
- **Evidence**: Review meeting minutes; version history

## A.3 — Internal organization

### A.3.2 AI roles and responsibilities

- **Objective**: Clear assignment of AI responsibilities
- **Implementation**: Named roles (AI Governance Lead, Model Owner, Ethics Committee chair)
- **Evidence**: RACI matrix; job descriptions; org chart

### A.3.3 Reporting of concerns

- **Objective**: Provide a means for personnel and others to report concerns
- **Implementation**: Reporting channel (ombuds, hotline, email); non-retaliation protection
- **Evidence**: Policy; usage logs (redacted)

## A.4 — Resources for AI systems

### A.4.2 Resource documentation

- **Objective**: Document AI resources (data, tools, systems, computing)
- **Implementation**: AI System Inventory; data catalog; compute inventory
- **Evidence**: Inventories with metadata

## A.5 — Assessing impacts of AI systems

### A.5.2 AI system impact assessment process

- **Objective**: Process for assessing potential consequences
- **Implementation**: Impact assessment template; trigger events (pre-deployment, annual, material change)
- **Evidence**: Documented process; completed assessments

### A.5.3 Documentation of impacts

- **Objective**: Document results of impact assessments
- **Implementation**: Standard impact assessment format; archive
- **Evidence**: Impact assessment records

### A.5.4 Assessing impacts on individuals

- **Objective**: Address impacts on individuals (privacy, rights, fairness)
- **Implementation**: Impact assessment includes individual-impact analysis; paired DPIA where applicable
- **Evidence**: Impact records; DPIAs

### A.5.5 Assessing societal impacts

- **Objective**: Address broader impacts (environmental, economic, democratic, safety)
- **Implementation**: Societal-impact section in impact assessments; consult stakeholders
- **Evidence**: Impact records; stakeholder consultation notes

## A.6 — AI system life cycle

### A.6.2.2 Objectives for responsible development

- **Objective**: Set objectives for responsible development
- **Implementation**: Documented objectives (fairness, transparency, safety, accountability)
- **Evidence**: Development objectives; reviews

### A.6.2.3 Processes for responsible design and development

- **Objective**: Establish development processes that implement responsible AI
- **Implementation**: Development methodology; reviews; approvals
- **Evidence**: Process documentation; review records

### A.6.2.4 Requirements and specifications

- **Objective**: Define and manage AI system requirements
- **Implementation**: Requirements capture; approval; change control
- **Evidence**: Requirements docs; change records

### A.6.2.5 Documentation of design and development

- **Objective**: Maintain documentation throughout development
- **Implementation**: Model cards; system cards; design decision logs
- **Evidence**: Documentation packages

### A.6.2.6 Verification and validation

- **Objective**: Verify and validate AI systems against requirements
- **Implementation**: Testing plan; bias testing; robustness testing; safety testing
- **Evidence**: Test plans; results; remediation records

### A.6.2.7 Deployment

- **Objective**: Plan and control deployment
- **Implementation**: Deployment procedure; rollback plans; communication
- **Evidence**: Deployment records; approvals

### A.6.2.8 Operation and monitoring

- **Objective**: Monitor AI systems in operation
- **Implementation**: Monitoring dashboards; alerts; review cadence
- **Evidence**: Monitoring records; issue logs

## A.7 — Data for AI systems

### A.7.2 Data for development and enhancement

- **Objective**: Ensure data used supports the AI system's intended purpose
- **Implementation**: Data requirements; selection criteria; suitability analysis
- **Evidence**: Data suitability analysis

### A.7.3 Acquisition of data

- **Objective**: Obtain data through permissible, documented means
- **Implementation**: Acquisition process; licenses; contracts; consent
- **Evidence**: Data acquisition records

### A.7.4 Quality of data

- **Objective**: Ensure data quality meets the needs of the AI system
- **Implementation**: Quality checks; validation; remediation
- **Evidence**: Data quality reports

### A.7.5 Data provenance

- **Objective**: Track origin, transformations, and lineage
- **Implementation**: Provenance tracking; metadata; versioning
- **Evidence**: Data lineage documentation

### A.7.6 Data preparation

- **Objective**: Prepare data (cleaning, labeling, augmenting) with documented process
- **Implementation**: Preparation procedures; annotation guidelines; quality control
- **Evidence**: Preparation documentation; annotation review

## A.8 — Information for interested parties

### A.8.2 System documentation and information for users

- **Objective**: Provide users with information to use the system effectively
- **Implementation**: User-facing documentation; instructions; limitations
- **Evidence**: Documentation; version control

### A.8.3 External reporting

- **Objective**: Report to external interested parties as appropriate
- **Implementation**: Public statements; trust-center content; customer reports
- **Evidence**: Public-facing docs; reports

### A.8.4 Communication of incidents

- **Objective**: Communicate AI-related incidents to interested parties
- **Implementation**: Incident communication plan; regulatory / customer notification
- **Evidence**: Communication records; notifications

### A.8.5 Information for interested parties

- **Objective**: Provide interested parties with sufficient information
- **Implementation**: Regulatory disclosures; customer DPAs; contract information; investor materials
- **Evidence**: Disclosures; contracts

## A.9 — Use of AI systems

### A.9.2 Processes for responsible use

- **Objective**: Ensure AI is used responsibly
- **Implementation**: Acceptable use policy; training; monitoring for misuse
- **Evidence**: Policy; training records; audit records

### A.9.3 Objectives for use

- **Objective**: Define objectives for use of AI
- **Implementation**: Business-owner-level statements of purpose for each system
- **Evidence**: Use-case documentation

### A.9.4 Intended use

- **Objective**: Define and communicate intended use
- **Implementation**: Intended-use statements; out-of-scope statements
- **Evidence**: System cards

## A.10 — Third-party relationships

### A.10.2 Allocation of responsibilities

- **Objective**: Allocate responsibilities between parties
- **Implementation**: Contracts; shared responsibility matrices
- **Evidence**: Contracts; SRMs

### A.10.3 Supplier relationships

- **Objective**: Manage AI-related risks in the supply chain
- **Implementation**: Vendor risk assessments; ongoing oversight
- **Evidence**: Vendor files; reviews

### A.10.4 Customer relationships

- **Objective**: Manage AI-related obligations to customers
- **Implementation**: DPAs; MSA AI clauses; customer notifications
- **Evidence**: Customer agreements; notification records

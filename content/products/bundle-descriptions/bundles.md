**Who it's for:** Businesses of 5-100 employees rolling out AI for the first time.

**Includes:**

- AI System Inventory template + instructions
- AI Procurement Checklist (Lite)
- AI Quick Reference Cards (all 6 role-specific cards)

**Why this bundle:** These are the five things every small business needs in its first 30 days of AI use. Starts you from zero with a policy your team signs, an inventory that tracks what you're actually using, a fast way to vet new tools, role-specific cards for each team, and disclosures for customer-facing AI.

**What you'll get done in week 1:** AUP published and signed. Approved tools list started. Employees know the rules. Chatbot and website disclosures live.

**Price:** $149-199 (vs. $250+ individually)

**Tagline:** Every hiring-AI rule, handled.

**Who it's for:** HR leaders, recruiters, people-ops teams at companies hiring across multiple U.S. jurisdictions.

**Includes:**
- AI in HR / Hiring Starter Pack (overview + HR addendum + workflow)

**Who it's for:** Companies operating in multiple U.S. states with AI-facing consumers.

**Includes:**

**Price:** $199-249 (vs. $300+ individually)

**Tagline:** The two big EU regimes, handled together.

**Who it's for:** Companies serving EU customers with AI features.

**Includes:**
- EU AI Act High-Risk Kit (overview + 16-phase checklist)
- GDPR ↔ EU AI Act Crosswalk
- AI Impact Assessment (GDPR DPIA + AI Act FRIA combined)
- AI DPA Standalone
- AI Subprocessor Management
- Customer AI Disclosure Templates
- AI Incident Response Plan (with Art 73 and GDPR 72h timelines)

**Why this bundle:** GDPR and the EU AI Act overlap significantly, but they're different regimes with different documents, different authorities, and different clocks. Running them as two separate programs wastes effort. This bundle turns them into one coherent program.

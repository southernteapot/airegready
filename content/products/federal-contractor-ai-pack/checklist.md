## Phase 1 — Scope

- [ ] **1.1** AI Systems that are sold to federal agencies
- [ ] **1.2** AI Systems used in performance of federal contracts
- [ ] **1.3** AI Systems that process federal data (PII, CUI, classified)
- [ ] **1.4** AI Systems used in federal-exposed hiring

## Phase 2 — OMB / NIST alignment

- [ ] **2.1** NIST AI RMF adoption documented
- [ ] **2.2** M-24-10 posture analyzed for systems sold to agencies
- [ ] **2.3** M-24-18 acquisition support materials prepared

## Phase 3 — FedRAMP (cloud)

- [ ] **3.1** FedRAMP authorization status for relevant systems
- [ ] **3.2** Baseline (Low / Moderate / High) appropriate
- [ ] **3.3** Continuous monitoring in place

## Phase 4 — Privacy Act / FISMA / CUI

- [ ] **4.1** Privacy Act compliance for PII
- [ ] **4.2** FISMA controls aligned with agency ATO
- [ ] **4.3** CUI marked, protected, and handled per DFARS 252.204-7012 and analogous
- [ ] **4.4** Classified handling where applicable

## Phase 5 — Section 508

- [ ] **5.1** VPAT prepared for each AI product sold to federal agencies
- [ ] **5.2** Accessibility testing completed
- [ ] **5.3** Alternative non-AI paths available

## Phase 6 — OFCCP

- [ ] **6.1** Hiring AI reviewed for OFCCP-compliant documentation
- [ ] **6.2** Adverse-impact analysis for affected groups
- [ ] **6.3** Records retained per OFCCP timelines

## Phase 7 — Export controls

- [ ] **7.1** Model / weight classification under EAR
- [ ] **7.2** ITAR classification if defense-related
- [ ] **7.3** Deemed-export review for foreign personnel
- [ ] **7.4** Cloud region compliance

## Phase 8 — Information packet

- [ ] **8.1** Federal AI info packet includes: NIST RMF statement, impact assessment, bias testing, model transparency, training data description, subprocessor list, incident commitments, kill switch, accessibility, human alternatives
- [ ] **8.2** Packet kept current

## Phase 9 — Subcontract flow-down

- [ ] **9.1** Sub-contract template updated with AI clauses
- [ ] **9.2** Flow-down review for existing subs
- [ ] **9.3** Subcontractor attestations / reporting established

## Phase 10 — Incident and reporting

- [ ] **10.1** Incident response plan aligned with contract reporting clocks
- [ ] **10.2** Contracting officer notifications for material AI issues
- [ ] **10.3** Cyber incident reporting under DFARS 252.204-7012 (if DoD CUI) and CIRCIA

## Phase 11 — Training

- [ ] **11.1** Cleared personnel trained on AI handling of classified / CUI
- [ ] **11.2** Program managers trained on federal AI procurement expectations
- [ ] **11.3** Engineers trained on export-control-aware model handling

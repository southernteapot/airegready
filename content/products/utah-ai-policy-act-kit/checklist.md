# Utah AI Policy Act — Readiness Checklist

**Organization:** ______________________________
**Completed by:** ______________________________
**Date:** ______________________________

## Phase 1 — Scope

- [ ] **1.1** Identified generative AI that reaches Utah consumers
- [ ] **1.2** Identified any DOPL-regulated occupations practicing in Utah that use generative AI
- [ ] **1.3** Documented AI roles: replacing a human, assisting a human, or fully automated

## Phase 2 — Regulated-occupation disclosure

- [ ] **2.1** For DOPL-regulated services using generative AI: prominent disclosure at the start of the interaction
- [ ] **2.2** Disclosure language reviewed by counsel
- [ ] **2.3** Delivery confirmed across channels (web, chat, voice, email)
- [ ] **2.4** Human oversight documented

## Phase 3 — Consumer disclosure

- [ ] **3.1** Chatbot / voice agent identifies itself as AI up front
- [ ] **3.2** Responds honestly if a consumer asks "Am I talking to a bot?"
- [ ] **3.3** Disclosure prominent and clear

## Phase 4 — Accountability

- [ ] **4.1** Output review process in place where AI interacts with consumers
- [ ] **4.2** Misrepresentation and deceptive-practices training for staff
- [ ] **4.3** Internal review of AI-generated claims (marketing, product info)
- [ ] **4.4** Clear line of accountability for AI-assisted decisions and communications

## Phase 5 — Sandbox (optional)

- [ ] **5.1** Use case evaluated for sandbox eligibility
- [ ] **5.2** Application prepared if pursuing
- [ ] **5.3** Reporting and monitoring capacity available
- [ ] **5.4** Legal review of sandbox terms

## Phase 6 — Recordkeeping

- [ ] **6.1** Disclosure language versioned and retained
- [ ] **6.2** Consumer complaint log
- [ ] **6.3** Internal review records for AI-assisted content
- [ ] **6.4** Sandbox records (if applicable) per Office requirements

## Phase 7 — Integration with other regimes

- [ ] **7.1** Utah Consumer Sales Practices Act compliance
- [ ] **7.2** Federal FTC alignment
- [ ] **7.3** Colorado AI Act if Colorado consumers also served
- [ ] **7.4** EU AI Act Art 50 if EU exposure
- [ ] **7.5** Sector rules (health, legal, financial) layered correctly

## Phase 8 — Monitoring

- [ ] **8.1** Track Utah Office of AI Policy announcements and enforcement
- [ ] **8.2** Annual review of disclosure language and operational practices
- [ ] **8.3** Legal update subscription for Utah AI developments

## Accuracy and reliability

### Is your AI always right?

No. AI can be wrong — sometimes confidently so. Our product includes:

- Human review where outputs are consequential
- Clear disclosures where AI is involved
- Error-reporting channels for you and your users

We expect users of AI-generated outputs to verify important facts before relying on them.

### What happens when your AI gets it wrong?

- If you catch an error, report it to [SUPPORT]
- We investigate and correct
- If the error affected a decision about someone, our incident response applies
- Appeals of AI-assisted decisions are handled by trained humans

### Do you monitor for bias?

Yes. We run bias testing before deploying AI features in sensitive workflows and on a schedule afterward. Summary results are available under NDA for enterprise customers.

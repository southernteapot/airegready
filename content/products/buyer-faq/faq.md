## Before you buy

### What are AIRegReady products?

Downloadable templates, policies, checklists, and kits you can adapt to your organization. Each product is a working document with `[BRACKETED PLACEHOLDERS]` that you fill in. Delivered as Word and PDF (Google Doc on request for some).

### Who are these for?

Most of our customers are small and mid-size businesses, startups, and nonprofits that need a defensible AI posture without an enterprise compliance team. Consultants, law firms, and agencies use our products under a Reseller License to accelerate work for their clients.

### Do I need a lawyer to use these?

Our products are written to be usable by non-lawyers. They are **templates for educational and informational purposes, not legal advice**. Before you adopt a policy for your organization, have qualified counsel review. Our products make that review cheaper and faster because the skeleton is already in place.

### How do your products compare to hiring a consultant?

A consultant will typically charge thousands of dollars to produce documents similar to ours. Our products give you a 70-80% starting point at a fraction of the cost. Many of our customers use our products plus a focused consultant engagement to polish for their specific situation.

### How do they compare to DIY (writing from scratch)?

Writing an AI Acceptable Use Policy from scratch is weeks of work for someone who doesn't do this regularly. Our AUP is ready to adapt in hours. Multiply that across a dozen policies and kits and the hours add up.

### Do you offer refunds?

Yes, under the circumstances in our [Refund Policy]. Short version: if the product isn't what the sales page said, email us within 14 days.

### What does my purchase cover?

A perpetual, single-company, non-transferable license for use within your organization. Full terms in the [Product License Agreement].

### Can I use a product with multiple entities in my corporate group?

The License covers entities within the same consolidated group (parent, subsidiaries, 50%+ common ownership). For entities outside that, you need separate licenses or a Reseller License.

### Can I use this at my consulting firm for multiple clients?

Yes, with a [Reseller License]. The standard single-company license does not cover consulting use across multiple unrelated clients.

### Can I modify the product?

Yes. Modification is the point. Fill in placeholders, add sections, remove sections, adapt to your organization.

### Can I share the product externally?

No. You cannot redistribute, resell, or publish the product (or substantial portions of it) externally. You can use the customized version of the product as your organization's policy, including sharing internal versions with employees and contractors.

### Can I use the product to train my own AI?

No. Our Terms and the Product License prohibit using our products to train AI models.

## Updates and maintenance

### Do I get updates?

Yes — **12 months of free updates** from the date of purchase. You'll be notified by email when updates are available.

### What counts as an update?

Regulatory updates, content improvements, new sections or templates added, new format additions, bundled additions. Details in our [Product Update Policy].

### What happens after 12 months?

You keep the product you have. For continued updates, you can:

- Renew updates for the specific product
- Subscribe to the Regulatory Update Service (covers all products you own)
- Do nothing (you keep your current version)

### What's the Regulatory Update Service?

A subscription that extends free updates, sends a monthly regulatory briefing on AI developments, and gives you change-log access. Useful if you're running an active AI program and want to stay current without re-buying products.

### How often are products updated?

As often as the law requires. State AI laws, EU rulemaking, and regulator guidance are moving fast — plan on at least a couple of updates per year for active regulatory products (CAIA kit, EU AI Act kit, etc.).

## Using the product

### Where do I start?

1. Open the product file
2. Read it through once
3. Fill in every `[BRACKETED PLACEHOLDER]`
4. Remove sections that don't apply
5. Have qualified counsel review
6. Publish internally and train your team

### How long does it take to implement?

Depends on the product. A single policy usually takes a few hours of customization plus review. A full kit (like the Colorado AI Act Readiness Kit) may take a few days of coordinated work across teams.

### I don't know what goes in [BRACKET]. What do I do?

If you're uncertain after reading the product, reach out to [SUPPORT]. For substantive legal questions, engage qualified counsel. Our products are designed to reduce the scope of what counsel needs to do, not eliminate it.

### Can I use your product names in my publications?

Limited use is fine (citing us as a source, crediting our framework). Do not reproduce substantial portions in external publications without permission.

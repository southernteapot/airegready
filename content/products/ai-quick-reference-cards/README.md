# AI Quick Reference Cards

One-pagers designed to be printed (or saved as a PDF) and kept nearby. Each card summarizes the most important AI rules for a specific role.

## Cards in this set

- `engineer-card.md`
- `marketing-card.md`
- `hr-card.md`
- `sales-card.md`
- `customer-support-card.md`
- `executive-card.md`

## Format

Each card is structured as:

- **The 5 rules (universal)**
- **What you can do (role-specific)**
- **What you can't do (role-specific)**
- **Traffic light data rules**
- **Scenarios for your role**
- **Who to ask**

## How to deploy

- Include in onboarding packets
- Pin in shared spaces (kitchen, break room, Slack channels)
- Distribute as PDF at training sessions
- Print for distributed / field teams

## Customization

Replace every `[BRACKETED]` placeholder with your company-specific details (approved tools, contact emails, SSO-login URL, etc.) before distributing.

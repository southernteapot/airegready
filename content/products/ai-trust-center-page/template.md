## Our approach to responsible AI

At [COMPANY NAME], we use artificial intelligence to make our products and services faster, smarter, and more helpful. This page describes how we build and operate AI responsibly, what we expect of our AI vendors, and what rights and protections you have when AI affects you.

We update this page when our practices change.

## Our principles

We commit to:

1. **Accountability**: A named human is responsible for every AI system we deploy.
2. **Transparency**: We know what AI we use, for what, and on what data — and we'll tell you what we can.
3. **Fairness**: We test and monitor for bias; we address what we find.
4. **Safety**: We test for misuse before deploying, and we monitor after.
5. **Privacy**: We minimize personal data in AI systems; we honor your rights.
6. **Human oversight**: People — not AI alone — decide things that matter.
7. **Continuous improvement**: We review our AI posture regularly and update as the technology and law evolve.

## Our AI Governance Program

We maintain an AI Governance Program that includes:

- **Named accountability** — AI Governance Lead and Executive Sponsor
- **Review process** — an AI Review Committee evaluates new AI systems before deployment
- **Impact assessments** — for AI systems that could materially affect people
- **Vendor risk management** — rigorous review of AI providers we work with
- **Training** — everyone on our team is trained on our AI policies
- **Incident response** — a playbook for handling AI-related issues
- **Framework alignment** — our program aligns to the NIST AI Risk Management Framework [and ISO/IEC 42001 / EU AI Act / other as applicable]

## Where we don't use AI

We don't use AI to:

- Make final hiring, lending, insurance, housing, or similar decisions without human review
- Infer your race, religion, sexual orientation, or other sensitive characteristics
- Monitor your emotional state
- Send content to you that falsely appears to be from a human when you'd reasonably expect a person

## Your data

**What we do with data you provide:**

- Process your data to provide our services
- Keep it secure per our [Security Overview]
- Retain it per our Privacy Policy
- Delete it per your request

**What we don't do:**

- We do not use your data to train AI models that serve other customers
- We do not sell your data
- We do not share your data with AI model providers who would use it for training

If a specific AI feature requires data for training or fine-tuning, we'll tell you and give you a choice.

- **Correct**: inaccurate data
- **Delete**: your data (subject to legal obligations)
- **Appeal**: adverse AI-assisted decisions to a human reviewer
- **Opt out** of certain automated processing (CCPA/CPRA, Colorado AI Act, GDPR)

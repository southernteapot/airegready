# Pre-Launch AI Risk Checklist

Purpose: Run a one-page check before you publish, sell, demo, advertise, onboard users, or deliver AI-assisted client work.

## When To Use It

Use this at the end of your launch prep, after you have completed a first pass through the project snapshot, claims checklist, data intake, red flag triage, disclosure builder, vendor note, incident log, and change log.

Use it again before major changes or relaunches.

## How To Fill It Out

Check each item only if it is true enough to rely on. Add notes for anything incomplete. If a high-risk issue is unresolved, pause or narrow the launch until it is reviewed.

## Review Carefully If

- Any checklist item is still incomplete but the project is about to be public, paid, or client-facing.
- A red-flag category is marked Yes or Unsure.
- The product handles sensitive data or affects real-world decisions.
- The public copy promises more than the AI workflow can support.

Short boundary: This worksheet is educational information, not legal advice. It is a starting point for documenting your AI project and spotting questions to review. Specific legal, privacy, contractual, or regulatory obligations should be reviewed with qualified counsel or the appropriate advisor.

# Customer FAQ Template

Purpose: Help you prepare clear answers to basic customer, client, or user questions about your AI use.

## When To Use It

Use this before launch, during onboarding, in sales conversations, on a support page, in a client proposal, or anywhere users may ask what the AI does and what its limits are.

## How To Fill It Out

Keep answers short and factual. Match them to your real workflow. Do not copy a template answer if it is not true for your project.

## Starter FAQ

### Do you use AI?

Template answer:

Yes. [PROJECT NAME] uses AI to [DRAFT / SUMMARIZE / CLASSIFY / SUGGEST / GENERATE] [TYPE OF OUTPUT]. AI is used as part of [FEATURE OR WORKFLOW].

If AI is only internal:

Yes. I use AI internally to help with [TASK], but I review the work before sending or publishing it.

### What does the AI do?

Template answer:

The AI helps [PLAIN DESCRIPTION OF TASK]. It uses [TYPE OF INPUT] to produce [TYPE OF OUTPUT]. It does not [IMPORTANT LIMIT OR THING IT DOES NOT DO].

### What data does the AI process?

Template answer:

The AI may process [TYPES OF DATA]. Please do not submit [DATA TO AVOID], such as [EXAMPLES], unless we have specifically agreed that it is appropriate.

If no personal data is intended:

The AI is designed to work with [NON-SENSITIVE INPUT TYPES]. Please avoid entering personal, sensitive, confidential, or private information.

### Do humans review the output?

Template answer when humans review output:

Yes. [I / WE / THE USER] review AI-generated output before [IT IS SENT / IT IS PUBLISHED / IT IS USED FOR FINAL WORK].

Template answer when output is user-reviewed:

The AI provides a draft or suggestion. You should review the output before using it, sharing it, or relying on it.

### Can I rely on the output?

Template answer:

Treat the output as a starting point, not a final answer. AI output can be incomplete, outdated, or incorrect. Check important details before using it.

### What should I not use this for?

Template answer:

Do not use this for [EXCLUDED USES]. It is not intended for legal, medical, financial, tax, mental health, safety, employment, housing, credit, insurance, or other professional or high-stakes decisions unless you have qualified review for your specific situation.

### How do I report a problem?

Template answer:

Contact [SUPPORT EMAIL OR FORM] with a short description of what happened. If possible, include the input, output, screenshot, date, and what you expected to happen.

### Can I opt out?

Template answer if opt-out exists:

Yes. You can [DESCRIBE OPT-OUT PATH]. If you opt out, [EXPLAIN WHAT CHANGES].

Template answer if opt-out does not exist:

There is not currently a separate non-AI version of this feature. If that is a concern, contact [SUPPORT EMAIL OR FORM] before using it.

### Does this replace professional advice?

Template answer:

No. This AI feature provides general drafting, summarizing, organizing, or support output. It does not replace qualified professional advice for your specific situation.

### How often does the system change?

Template answer:

The AI feature may change when we update prompts, workflows, vendors, models, data settings, or product behavior. We keep a change log for meaningful updates and review user-facing disclosures when changes affect what users should know.

## Review Carefully If

- A customer could treat the answer as a promise, guarantee, legal term, privacy notice, or professional advice.
- Your FAQ mentions data use, sensitive information, children, regulated topics, or opt-out rights.
- The FAQ no longer matches the actual product behavior.

Short boundary: This worksheet is educational information, not legal advice. It is a starting point for documenting your AI project and spotting questions to review. Specific legal, privacy, contractual, or regulatory obligations should be reviewed with qualified counsel or the appropriate advisor.

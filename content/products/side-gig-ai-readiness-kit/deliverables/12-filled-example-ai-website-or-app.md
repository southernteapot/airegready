# Filled Example: AI Website Or App

Purpose: Show how the Solo Builder AI Launch Kit can look for a realistic AI-powered website or app.

## When To Use It

Use this example when your project is a website, no-code app, lightweight SaaS feature, wrapper, or user-facing AI tool.

## How To Read It

This is fictional. Copy the level of detail, not the exact facts. Your project may need different disclosures, review steps, or qualified help.

## Example Project

Project: LaunchPad Copy Lab

Scenario: A solo builder launches a paid web app that helps small creators draft first-pass landing-page copy from a short intake form.

The app is not intended to provide legal, financial, medical, or professional advice. It drafts marketing copy ideas that users review and edit before publishing.

## Customer FAQ Excerpt

### Do you use AI?

Yes. LaunchPad Copy Lab uses AI to draft landing-page copy ideas from the product details you provide.

### Can I publish the output as-is?

You should review and edit the output before publishing it. AI can miss context, invent details, or suggest claims that do not fit your product.

### What should I not use this for?

Do not use it to create legal terms, professional advice, regulated claims, guaranteed earnings claims, or copy for products you cannot accurately describe or support.

## Review Carefully If

- The app starts making stronger conversion, revenue, compliance, or professional-advice claims.
- Users can upload client files, customer lists, or sensitive data.
- The AI starts publishing copy automatically without user review.

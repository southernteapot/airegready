# Incident / Complaint Mini-Log

Purpose: Track user complaints, bad outputs, privacy concerns, or AI-related issues so they do not disappear into inboxes, chat threads, or memory.

## When To Use It

Use this whenever someone reports an incorrect, confusing, harmful, biased, unexpected, privacy-related, or AI-related issue. Also use it for near misses, internal discoveries, client complaints, platform notices, and support tickets.

## How To Fill It Out

Add a row as soon as you learn about the issue. You can update the row later as you investigate, respond, fix, or close it.

## Issue Types

Use these labels or create your own:

- Incorrect output
- Harmful or unsafe output
- Biased or offensive output
- User confusion
- Privacy or data concern
- Client confidential data concern
- Disclosure concern
- Unsupported claim
- Vendor or outage issue
- Prompt or model behavior issue
- Security or access issue
- Other

## Response Notes

For each meaningful issue, capture:

- What happened?
- Who was affected?
- What data was involved?
- Was the user, client, or customer notified?
- Was any public copy, disclosure, prompt, workflow, or vendor setting updated?
- Does the issue suggest a broader pattern?
- Should the project be paused, narrowed, or reviewed by someone qualified?

## Escalate Or Get Help If

- The issue involves sensitive personal data, client confidential information, credentials, payment data, or children.
- The AI output may have affected a real-world decision.
- A user claims harm, financial loss, discrimination, privacy harm, or reliance on incorrect output.
- The issue involves legal, medical, mental health, financial, employment, housing, credit, education, insurance, or safety topics.
- The same issue happens more than once.
- You are unsure whether to notify someone, preserve records, pause a feature, or change data handling.

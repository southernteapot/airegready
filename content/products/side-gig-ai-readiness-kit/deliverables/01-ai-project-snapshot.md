# AI Project Snapshot

Purpose: Capture the basic facts about your AI project so the launch does not live only in your head, codebase, prompts, or sales copy.

## When To Use It

Use this worksheet when you are planning, prototyping, launching, selling, demoing, or changing an AI-enabled app, website, automation, consulting workflow, digital product, or client deliverable.

Use it again whenever the AI feature, model, vendor, data flow, public claims, or user-facing behavior changes.

## How To Fill It Out

Write short, plain answers. If you do not know something yet, write Unknown or TBD. The point is to make gaps visible, not to produce a perfect document.

## Review Carefully If

- You are making accuracy, earnings, safety, professional-advice, or business-outcome claims.
- The AI touches sensitive personal data, client confidential information, children or minors, credentials, payment data, or regulated subject matter.
- The AI affects real-world decisions about people.
- Users may rely on outputs without human review.
- You changed vendors, models, prompts, data sources, or public copy since the last review.

Short boundary: This worksheet is educational information, not legal advice. It is a starting point for documenting your AI project and spotting questions to review. Specific legal, privacy, contractual, or regulatory obligations should be reviewed with qualified counsel or the appropriate advisor.

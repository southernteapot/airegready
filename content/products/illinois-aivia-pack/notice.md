# AIVIA — Candidate Notice and Consent Template

Ready-to-adapt language for candidate notice, explanation, and consent required by the Illinois AI Video Interview Act. Have employment counsel review before use.

---

## Template — sent before scheduling the video interview

> **Subject:** About your upcoming video interview for [POSITION TITLE]
>
> Dear [CANDIDATE NAME],
>
> Thank you for applying to [COMPANY NAME] for the [POSITION TITLE] role. We're writing to let you know that, if you agree, your video interview for this role may be analyzed using artificial intelligence.
>
> This notice explains: (1) what the AI does, (2) what it evaluates, (3) what happens to your video, and (4) your choices.
>
> **(1) What the AI does**
>
> We use an AI tool called **[VENDOR NAME / TOOL NAME]** to help our recruiting team review video interviews. The AI reviews your responses to our interview questions and summarizes what it hears — for example, whether your answers describe experience with the skills listed for this role.
>
> The AI does **not** make the final hiring decision. A human recruiter and hiring team review your interview before any decision is made.
>
> **(2) What the AI evaluates**
>
> The AI evaluates the **content of your responses** to our interview questions. Specifically, it looks at:
>
> - [Relevant skills and experience mentioned in your answers]
> - [Examples of work you describe]
> - [Specific qualifications the role requires]
>
> The AI **does not** evaluate your facial expressions, emotions, or personality traits. *[Adjust if your tool does any of this; see notes below.]*
>
> **(3) What happens to your video**
>
> Your video will be:
>
> - Stored securely on systems controlled by [COMPANY NAME] and [VENDOR NAME]
> - Shared only with individuals involved in evaluating your application, and with the AI vendor that provides the tool
> - **Destroyed within 30 days of your request** if you ask us to destroy it
>
> **(4) Your choices**
>
> - **If you consent**, your video interview will be analyzed by the AI as described.
> - **If you do not consent**, we will arrange a **human-only interview** for this role. You will not be disadvantaged for declining AI analysis.
> - You can ask us to **destroy your video** at any time by emailing [CAREERS EMAIL]. We will confirm destruction within 30 days.
>
> **Please indicate your choice** by [replying to this email / clicking one of the options below] by [DATE] so we can schedule your interview.
>
> ☐ **I consent** to AI analysis of my video interview as described above.
> ☐ **I do not consent**; please arrange a human-only interview.
>
> If you have questions, please contact [HR CONTACT NAME] at [EMAIL] or [PHONE].
>
> Thank you,
>
> [RECRUITER NAME]
> [COMPANY NAME]

---

## If your tool analyzes facial / voice / emotional signals

Revise the "(2) What the AI evaluates" section to disclose this clearly. Example:

> The AI evaluates the content of your responses. It also analyzes [audio features such as speaking rate and clarity / visual features such as the presence of a face in frame and general engagement indicators]. It does not attempt to infer emotions or personality traits.

If your tool attempts to infer emotions or personality, we recommend not using that capability for hiring. See the AIVIA overview for why (EU AI Act prohibitions, Title VII / ADA concerns, litigation risk).

## Destruction request — email template (from applicant)

> To: [CAREERS EMAIL]
> Subject: Request to destroy my interview video
>
> Dear [COMPANY NAME],
>
> I'm writing to request that you destroy the video interview I participated in on [DATE] for the [POSITION TITLE] role. Please confirm destruction per the Illinois AI Video Interview Act.
>
> My contact information:
>
> - Name: [APPLICANT NAME]
> - Email: [EMAIL]
> - Application reference (if known): [REFERENCE]
>
> Thanks,
> [APPLICANT NAME]

## Destruction confirmation — response template

> Dear [APPLICANT NAME],
>
> We received your request to destroy the video interview from [DATE] for the [POSITION TITLE] role. We've initiated the destruction process.
>
> We will confirm complete destruction of all copies — including those held by our AI vendor — within 30 days of your request. You'll receive a follow-up from us.
>
> If you have any questions, reach us at [CAREERS EMAIL].
>
> Thanks,
>
> [HR CONTACT]
> [COMPANY NAME]

# AIVIA — Readiness Checklist

**Employer:** [NAME]
**Completed by:** ______________________________
**Date:** ______________________________

---

## Phase 1 — Scope

- [ ] **1.1** Identified video-interview AI tools in use
- [ ] **1.2** Confirmed which roles are Illinois-based or accept Illinois candidates
- [ ] **1.3** Documented: tool, vendor, features, what AI evaluates
- [ ] **1.4** Determined whether tool is used solely to filter for in-person interviews (triggers reporting)
- [ ] **1.5** Face / voice / emotion analysis usage flagged for BIPA review

## Phase 2 — Notice and consent

- [ ] **2.1** Written notice template reviewed by employment counsel
- [ ] **2.2** Notice delivered before interview scheduling
- [ ] **2.3** Explanation of AI function in plain language
- [ ] **2.4** Consent captured explicitly (not bundled with other consents)
- [ ] **2.5** Alternative (human-only) interview path available and communicated

## Phase 3 — Video handling

- [ ] **3.1** Sharing limited to those with evaluation or technology expertise
- [ ] **3.2** Vendor contract limits sharing consistent with AIVIA
- [ ] **3.3** Storage security consistent with AIVIA and BIPA

## Phase 4 — Destruction

- [ ] **4.1** Channel for destruction requests (email, form)
- [ ] **4.2** 30-day destruction SLA implemented
- [ ] **4.3** Vendor destruction coordinated
- [ ] **4.4** Confirmation sent to applicant
- [ ] **4.5** Record of destruction retained (but not the video itself)

## Phase 5 — Demographic reporting (if solely AI)

- [ ] **5.1** Demographic data collected lawfully
- [ ] **5.2** Annual reporting submitted to Illinois DCEO per statute
- [ ] **5.3** Internal review of reporting prior to submission

## Phase 6 — BIPA overlay (if face / voice analysis)

- [ ] **6.1** BIPA notice provided
- [ ] **6.2** BIPA consent captured
- [ ] **6.3** BIPA-compliant written policy published
- [ ] **6.4** Retention schedule meets BIPA requirements (destruction when purpose is satisfied, or within one year)

## Phase 7 — Illinois HRA (effective 2026)

- [ ] **7.1** Notice to employees and applicants when AI used in employment decisions
- [ ] **7.2** Evaluation of AI for discriminatory effect on protected classes
- [ ] **7.3** Confirmation that ZIP code is not used as proxy for race in AI employment decisions

## Phase 8 — Vendor management

- [ ] **8.1** Vendor risk assessment completed
- [ ] **8.2** Vendor contract flow-down (AIVIA, BIPA, HRA obligations)
- [ ] **8.3** Vendor supports demographic reporting where applicable
- [ ] **8.4** Vendor supports destruction workflow

## Phase 9 — Training

- [ ] **9.1** Recruiters and hiring managers trained
- [ ] **9.2** Applicant-facing support trained on notice, consent, and destruction handling

## Phase 10 — Records

- [ ] **10.1** Notice versions retained
- [ ] **10.2** Consents retained
- [ ] **10.3** Destruction records retained
- [ ] **10.4** Demographic reporting retained per statute
